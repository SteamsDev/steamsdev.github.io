OpenRA Lua Tutorial

If you ever wondered how to get started with making custom campaigns/scripted maps in OpenRA and want a starting point, this tutorial aims to show you the basics and get you started on the path to making great custom maps like mine! Many of the tips featured here come from experience, but there are plenty of sources referenced here that you'll also want to check out after this turorial to get a full understanding of making maps in OpenRA.
