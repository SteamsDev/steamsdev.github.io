Advanced Tips and Tricks

This section is for some of the advanced tips and tricks for making better OpenRA scripts. While note required to make a great map, they can make the quality of the mpa a lot better and help with some things that wouldn't be possibe to do otherwise, and some of these are just fun to mess around with and improve upon to make even better maps. As always, to see more and better applications of these tricks, take a look at some of the pre-packaged singleplayer maps that OpenRA comes with as standard.
