Dayspring: Dynasties

Dayspring: Dynasties is the first short story series I've started that takes place in my own fictional world. THe name is inspired by the high school I went to, and many of tht characters in the story are based on real-life people and places I've been. It's also the first time I'm going to use AI as a means of fleshing out the story, illustrating parts of the world I'm creating, and coming up with new ideas. Of course, the storyies themselves will be written by me.
